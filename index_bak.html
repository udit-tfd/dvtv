OUR PROCESS

  1. Initial Concept

    You come to us with an idea or goal or your brand strategy. We develop a creative brief based on your expected results meaning you get to visualise the end result on paper and be happy with it before we go into production.

  2. Shooting Plan & Schedule

    We plan every aspect of the shoot giving great considerations to location, time, weather, style, transporation, health & safety, equipment, designs, sound, graphics and so on.

  3. Production & Casting Crews

    We work in collaboration with some of the top professionals in the Irish film industry with the unfailing support of some of the best casting agencies in the country. We support and promote Ireland-based casting and production crews by providing great opportunities to help them leverage their careers.

  4. Post Production

    We edit the footage into something sublime, before proceeding to colour correction, VFX, audio mix, sound design, and titles.

  5. Completion & Delivery

    After you have signed off on all the above stage and you are completely happy with the final edits, we will deliver the final video in all agreed formats to fit every platform on which you wish to share with the world.
